Welcome to the world of Pokémon

Pokémon Red Version is a 1996 role-playing video game developed by Game Freak and published by Nintendo for the Game Boy.
This is the first installment of the Pokémon video game series. This was first released in Japan in 1996 as Pocket Monsters: Red with the special edition Pocket Monsters: Blue being released in Japan later that same year. The games were later released as Pokémon Red and Pokémon Blue in North America and Australia in 1998 and Europe in 1999. Pokémon Red and Pokémon Blue combined Red/Green/Blue for release outside of Japan.

Start your Journey by answering these:

Who are you?

You can choose one of them to start your journey:

  • Charmander - The Fire type Starter

    • From the time it is born, a flame burns at the tip of its tail. Its life would end if the flame were to go out.

  • Squirtle - The Water type Starter

    • When it feels threatened, it draws its limbs inside its shell and sprays water from its mouth.

  • Bulbasaur - The Grass type Starter

    • While it is young, it uses the nutrients that are stored in the seed on its back in order to grow.

Whom do you choose?

To complete this game, you have to become the Champion of Kanto Region by winning the Indigo League

These are the 8 Gyms that you need to defeat in order to participate in Indigo League:

  1. Pewter City Gym - The Rock type Gym [Gym Leader-Brock]
  2. Cerulean City Gym - The Water type Gym [Gym Leader-Misty]
  3. Vermilion City Gym - The Electric type Gym [Gym Leader-Lt. Surge]
  4. Celadon City Gym - The Grass type Gym [Gym Leader-Erika]
  5. Fuchsia City Gym - The Bug type Gym [Gym Leader-Koga]
  6. Saffron City Gym - The Psychic type Gym [Gym Leader-Sabrina]
  7. Cinnabar Island Gym - The Fire type Gym [Gym Leader-Blaine]
  8. Viridian City Gym - The Ground type Gym [Gym Leader-Giovanni]

After you get the Gym Badges from all the 8 Gyms, you are eligible for challenging the Elite Four of the Indigo League.

The Elite Four:

  1. Lorelei - The Water and Ice type user
  2. Bruno - The Rock and Fighting type user
  3. Agatha - The Ghost and Poison type user
  4. Lance - The Dragon type user

After you've beaten all the Elite Four members, your strongest rival will appear before you as the Champion.
You have to beat him next to become the New Champion of the Indigo Leaugue.

  • Your biggest rival and the current Champion - Blue

Congratulations!!You are crowned as the New Champion.
